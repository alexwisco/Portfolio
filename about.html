My name is Alex Wisco, and I am a software developer from Canada. I am currently working as a software/web developer with a startup company Biggabed , GM of Recruitment for Top Draft Hockey , and freelance web developer.

I graduated with a degree in Computer Science from Amherst College. I played Varsity hockey and was named All-Conference Academic team all four years. Before Amherst, I played two years for the Navan Grads in the CCHL in Ottawa, Ontario. I set the Grads franchise record for most career and single season shutouts (2018).

Although most of my background in programming and computer science is more academia-based, I've recently found myself learning and practicing front-end development more and more everyday. I started combining my knowledge of mathematical/computer science principles with my passion for front-end development and web design, which can be found on my projects page. I hope to continue to hone my fundamentals in data structures, algorithms, while also growing as a front-end developer.

I have previously worked as a Robotics and Coding instructor at Zebra Robotics in Toronto, a Data Analyst for DataForce, and I have 10+ years experience as a hockey goaltending coach. Outside of coding, my hobbies include finding new music, playing videogames, hiking/birdwatching, and working out.
